Presenter Notes

  • IDE Plugins: Best for real-time coding assistance and maintaining flow
  • Terminal Tools: Excellent for bulk operations and automation scripts
  • Web Interfaces: Great for exploration, planning, and collaborative discussions
  • APIs: Most flexible but require more setup - best for custom workflows
  • Choose based on your workflow, not the hype around specific tools
