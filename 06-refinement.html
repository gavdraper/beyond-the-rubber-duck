üéØ Real-time Feed Notifications
Story
As a social media user,
I want to see a notification when new posts are available
so that I can choose to refresh my feed without missing recent content.
Acceptance Criteria
Given new posts exist,
when I'm viewing the feed,
then a notification banner appears at the top
Evidence: Component

Given no new posts are available,
when I'm viewing the feed,
then no notification banner should be displayed
Evidence: Unit

Given notification banner is already visible,
when additional new posts arrive,
then banner remains visible without duplicating
Evidence: Unit

Given notification is displayed,
when I press the period (.) key,
then feed scrolls to top and refreshes posts
Evidence: E2E
